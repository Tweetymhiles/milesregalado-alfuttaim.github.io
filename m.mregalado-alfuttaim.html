Working with Al Futtaim Orient Insurance, one of the most reputable insurance companies in the UAE.


As a part of the reputable Al-Futtaim Group, Orient Insurance Company began operations in 1982 and has grown steadily ever since. Orient Insurance Company holds the highest credit rating in the UAE's insurance sector, an A+, and has paid up capital of AED 500 million. In addition to its internet sales channels and electronic services, the company serves its clientele through a vast network of branches located in Jebel Ali, Abu Dhabi, Al Ain, Sharjah, and Ras Al Khaimah in the United Arab Emirates, as well as Muscat in the Sultanate of Oman and Bahrain.

Additionally, the company holds an ISO 9001 Quality Management Certification. The Company makes a significant economic contribution to the United Arab Emirates by actively participating in large-scale, historic projects there.

Orient Insurance Company has over 40 years of expertise in the UAE insurance sector and is a customer-focused insurer. The company is prepared to offer creative risk solutions to the complete pleasure of its clients since it fully comprehends their needs.

The company's strong financial foundation, highly skilled and amiable staff, extensive understanding of the regional insurance market, and varied product offering, enhanced by a robust panel of elite European reinsurers, are its key competitive advantages.
